    Booster Pack: Glowing Mushrooms is just that, a small content pack for the early game glowing mushroom biome!

    This mod contains 8 new high quality weapons (2 per class) and a new armor set, all themed around the glowing mushroom biome. Booster Pack: Glowing Mushroom aims to be as balenced and nonintrusive as posible so that it can be used in tandem with larger mods.
